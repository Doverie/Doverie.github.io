sketcher

History behind the scenes

Sketcher is a simple tool that uses Convolutional Neural Networks to recognize drawings. The CNN was trained to recognize 100 classes using the quick draw dataset. It achieves 6% top 5 error. The accuracy could be improved although model is super lightweighted with around 1 million parameters. The table will show the top 5 predictions.
